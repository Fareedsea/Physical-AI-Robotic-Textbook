# Practical Example: The Intuitive da Vinci Surgical Robot Safety and Ethics

## Introduction to the Example

In this practical example, we'll examine the Intuitive da Vinci Surgical Robot, a medical robot that exemplifies the critical importance of safety and ethics in robotics. The da Vinci system demonstrates how rigorous safety standards, ethical considerations, and human oversight are essential in high-stakes applications where human lives are at risk.

## Overview of the da Vinci Surgical Robot

The da Vinci Surgical System is a sophisticated robotic platform designed to enable surgeons to perform complex procedures with enhanced precision, flexibility, and control. Developed by Intuitive Surgical, the system has been used in millions of procedures worldwide and represents one of the most successful implementations of robotics in healthcare.

### Key Specifications:
- **Setup**: Patient-side cart, surgeon console, vision cart
- **Robotic Arms**: 3-4 robotic arms with EndoWrist instruments
- **3D Vision**: High-definition 3D visualization system
- **Controls**: Master controls that translate surgeon movements
- **Safety Systems**: Multiple redundant safety mechanisms
- **FDA Approval**: Approved for various surgical procedures
